# Distributed monolith

> Distributed monoliths typically emerge in an environment where not enough focus was placed on concepts like information hiding and cohesion of business functional‐ ity, leading instead to highly coupled architectures in which changes ripple across service boundaries, and seemingly innocent changes that appear to be local in scope break other parts of the system.
